The most interesting fact I ever learned from research was...*
Coulomb's Law. Coulomb’s Law appeared numerous times in both my physics and chemistry classes, playing a crucial role in illustrating the behaviors between charges. It’s fascinating to see how the equation can explain phenomena from electric fields to electrostatic force and atomic radius. Learning this law opened me to the world of using equations to explain natural phenomena and motivated me to set out on a learning adventure, including AP Physics and AP Chemistry this year.
