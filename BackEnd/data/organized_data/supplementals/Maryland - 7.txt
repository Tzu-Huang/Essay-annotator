Content: I almost successfully hatched chickens with a handmade incubator I constructed with a cardboard box. My dad had brought home a carton of eggs that he was expecting to cook for dinner, but I, being the adventurous kid I was, decided I would hatch these eggs. I first found a big cardboard box and surrounded the sides with towels to act as insulators. Then, I went to the hardware store to buy a 20W incandescent bulb to act as the heat source. After a few modifications to the box, I assembled all the parts and waited for the eggs to hatch. Every day, I checked the humidity and temperature levels, but on the 21st day, a power outage ended it all.
