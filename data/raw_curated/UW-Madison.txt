Why is the University of Wisconsin–Madison a strong fit for you and your college goals?
        UW-Madison's strong engineering program in combination with its famed sports culture are what continue to draw me to UW-Madison. In the past few months, I have become a starter on the Volleyball team and earned a ski coach certification while continuing my leadership role on the basketball team, planning and going on cycling trips with my team, and training the junior volleyball players who will take my place next year. Sports are a huge part of my life: it would be amazing to fully immerse myself with UW-Madison’s sports vibes and be the #1 fan at all of the games.
        My previous engineering experience has been mostly focused on software and game development, but as I’ve been taking AP Physics and Statistics this year, I’m also interested in branching out and exploring other kinds of engineering such as Mechanical or Aerospace. UW-Madison’s many programs and the students and professors doing work across these many disciplines would be most helpful to guiding me to my ultimate engineering destination, and finding success in that field.
With its large student population and many programs, UW-Madison is where I believe I can settle on my path and be among peers who share the same passion for sports that I do. I look forward to experiencing the college experience fully, and there would be no better place to do so than UW-Madison.
