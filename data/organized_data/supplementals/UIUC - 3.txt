Explain, in detail, an experience you've had in the past 3 to 4 years related to your first-choice major. This can be an experience from an extracurricular activity, in a class you've taken, or through something else. (150)

Earlier this year, I competed in the national Geography Olympiad. My academic knowledge carried me through the questions and writing assignments of the local preliminaries and semi-final. However, the final round required real field research. My team studied the impact of the coal mining industry and fluvial erosion on the residents of Xindian. We explored various trails and visited local towns and tourist destinations. By examining land use patterns, we determined the former economic importance of the now-closed mines. Afterwards, I helped develop plans to establish and enhance flood protection in the area. I also devised several methods to preserve and stimulate cultural heritage sites through increased tourism to increase their economic potential. We received a bronze medal for our efforts, and I was nominated for the International Olympiad. Hopefully, I will get to represent my country on the global stage and continue my study of physical and human geography.
