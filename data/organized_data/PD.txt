Briefly discuss your reasons for pursuing the alternate major you have selected. (Respond in 250 words or fewer.)

From reading articles on psychological disorders to analyzing case studies, I have discovered my interest in researching the biological base of human behavior at Purdue University and learning about treatments to address their conditions.
