Describe your personal and/or career goals after graduating from UIUC and how your selected first-choice major will help you achieve them.
With my career goals centered around making advancements in the semiconductor industry that will benefit the transmission speed of data in digital devices, I aspire to work for a company that focuses on sustainable solutions, where I can play a role in designing and implementing such innovative projects. Through SED and its unique Secondary Field Option in Environmental Quality, I wish to integrate my passion for technology with my goal of tackling climate issues and sustaining the human population. Courses like CEE 330-Environmental Engineering could equip me with environmental aspects to consider when carrying out unit operations. Besides building a solid foundation for my intended career through technical core and electives, such as ECE 110-Introduction to Electronics and SE 320-Control Systems, by joining practical laboratory experiences in the Holonyak Micro & Nanotechnology Lab, research projects, and internship opportunities, I hope to get hands-on while getting myself involved in the industry.
